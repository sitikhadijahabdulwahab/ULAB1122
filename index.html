ULAB1122,

Academic English Skill

This course emphasizes the four language skills. It focuses on developing students' productive and receptive skills through student-centred activities in academic situations. This includes reading academic texts, listening for main ideas and details, taking notes, writing clearly and coherently, and participating in class discussions and oral presentation. Additionally, enrichment grammar activities are also incorporated to integrate the skills and knowledge. At the end ofthis course, students should be able touse the English language in daily and academic activities.
